About

I’m an open-minded and curious individual who enjoys collaborative environments where learning and achieving great results as a team is a central element. I believe in organizations without much emphasis on hierarchy and instead where freedom, responsibility, leadership and results are key. I’m very results-oriented, self-motivated, and have strong problem solving, organizational and planning skills.

My main driving force is to build and improve things – myself and everything I’m involved in. I enjoy brainstorming and finding solutions to problems ranging from everyday problems to advanced technical ones. One of my best skills is looking at the big picture and connecting the dots. To me this means understanding cause and effect in complex systems. This allows for making good decisions and finding optimal solutions. Part of it is to understand what the right problems to be solved are, so the right considerations and trade-offs can be made to deliver something truly valuable. This caters for innovation and exploration of new ideas, thinking outside normal boundaries and trying to break current assumptions. I often question the status quo to see what can be made simpler and better.

In software engineering, I have a versatile skill set and I am an expert in designing, architecting and building large-scale distributed software systems. I’ve had a central role in several large software projects for UNIX, Linux and Windows in both telecom and financial services. Furthermore, I’m experienced with web technologies and building modern web sites as well as utilizing open source software as part of overall solutions. I follow key open source projects and have participated with fixes on occasion.

I have a strong professional interest in automation, networking, distributed systems, Internet technologies and building great products, finding simple and innovative solutions to real problems. In addition, I have a keen interest in learning new things and continuously work on own projects where it is possible to explore new ideas and technologies.

I consider myself an expert in Agile methodologies and what their mission is about.

Considered to be very detail-oriented, honest, trustworthy, forward-thinking, helpful and technically savvy.

Cheers, Tommy
